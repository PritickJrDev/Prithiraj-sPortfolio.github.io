About Me

Hi! i'm Prithiraj, a self-motivated game developer from India, and i've been making games in Unity Engine since 2 years. Most of my works are mainly focus on coding and integration of different tools and features like Audio Source, Particle system, Animation and Animator, Managing assets, Game objects and Components etc. within the unity system to create a fully playable game. i'm also interested in solving problems, stroy telling and execute creative ideas to make a game more fun and enjoyable.
i believe, one day AAA title Indian games will be a reality and to be a part of the team will be a great achievement
